Top Travel Destinations in Kerala

Kerala, known as gods own country is blessed with some extremely beautiful tourist destinations that grab the attention of tourists from all across the globe. It is a land of hill-stations backwaters, commercial cities, hamlets, rich cultures and traditions, and much more to explore. While you may be wondering where to head first in the charming land of Kerala; do not worry, just scroll down to know what all awaits you here!

Top Attractions in Kerala

Kerala Backwaters

The Kerala backwaters are a chain of brackish lagoons and lakes lying parallel to the Arabian Sea coast are one of the ultimate eye-catching attractions or an important feature of Tourism in Kerala.

Kerala Hill Stations

Kerala, engulfed by the western Ghat ranges on its eastern border, unarguably has a wonderful collection of hill stations and lot of pleasure to offer

Kerala Wildlife

Kerala which is often well-known for scenic backwaters, beaches and Ayurveda’s therapy is also bestowed with opulent wildlife that extent above the length and width of Kerala.

Kerala Beaches:

Kerala, with an amazing 550 kilometers of coastline on the Arabian Sea has many pleasant or charming beaches and no wonder are a good alternative to Goa.

Temples of Kerala

Kerala is also known as “Land of Gods” as the land is full of legends & tales and houses several ancient as well as magnificent temples. Some of the temples are 2000 years old.

Fair and Festivals in Kerala

There are many local festivals of Kerala which show the rich and distinct culture of the God's Own Country by mixing cheer, fun and pleasure.
